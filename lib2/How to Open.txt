1. Open Ubuntu and Search for "Terminal"

2. In the Terminal write commands-
   a) vi file-name.l
   b) Press i and Enter [i is for Insert]

3. Now write codes (Or Copy-Paste from Text Folder)

4. After writting Press Esc key on Keyborad
5. First Press Shift+; and then type wq (:wq)
   and press Enter

6. Then write commands-
   a) flex file-name.l
   b) gcc lex.yy.c -lfl -o file-name
   c) ./file-name 
   d) Write output codes
   e) Then press Ctrl+D
